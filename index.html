Privacy Policy for AlarmAI

Last updated: December 01, 2025

Introduction

AlarmAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AlarmAI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Your Name: When you first use the app, we ask for your name to personalize wake-up messages. This name is stored only on your device.
  • Alarm Data: Alarm times, labels, repeat settings, vibration preferences, snooze settings, and ringtone choices you create. All alarm data is stored locally on your device.
  • AI Prompts: Optional custom prompts you provide for personalized wake-up messages.
  • Bug Reports (Optional): If you choose to submit bug reports, we collect:
    • Your bug description
    • Screenshots (if you choose to attach them)
    • Device information (model, OS version)
    • Timestamp

Automatically Collected Information

We do not automatically collect any personal information, analytics, or tracking data from your device.

Permissions We Request

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Required to display alarm notifications. You are asked for this permission on first launch (Android 13+).
  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM): Required to trigger alarms at precise times.
  • Internet Access (INTERNET): Required only for:
    • Generating AI wake-up messages (optional feature)
    • Submitting bug reports (optional, only when you choose to report)
  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK): Keeps device awake when alarm rings
  • Vibrate (VIBRATE): Provides vibration alerts for alarms
  • Full Screen Intent (USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT): Displays full-screen alarm when device is locked
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE): Ensures alarm rings even when app is in background
Note: The app does NOT request or use:
  • Location permissions
  • Camera permissions (screenshots for bug reports use system screenshot feature)
  • Contacts access
  • SMS/Phone permissions
  • Storage permissions (beyond standard app storage)

How We Use Your Information

Data Stored on Your Device (Local Only)

  • Your Name: Stored in app preferences, used to personalize AI messages
  • Alarm Data: Stored locally to manage your alarms
  • Language Preference: Your chosen language for AI messages

Data Sent to Third Parties

Google Gemini AI (Optional Feature)

When you use AI-powered wake-up messages, the following information is sent to Google's Gemini API:

  • Your custom prompt or selected topic (e.g., "motivational", "fitness")
  • Your name (if you enable "Say My Name" option)
  • Selected language (Hindi, English, or Spanish)

What Gemini AI does:

  • Generates a personalized motivational message
  • Converts it to audio using text-to-speech
  • Returns the audio to your device

We do NOT store these messages. They are generated in real-time and played once during the alarm.

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information about how Google processes this data.

Firebase Firestore (Optional - Bug Reports Only)

If you choose to submit a bug report, the following is sent to Firebase Firestore:

  • Your bug description
  • Screenshot (if attached)
  • Device model and OS version
  • Timestamp
  • App version

This is completely optional. You choose when to submit bug reports. We use this data solely to fix bugs and improve the app.

Please review Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT collect usage statistics
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Data: All alarm data and your name are stored only on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences and local storage
  • Cloud Data: Only bug reports you voluntarily submit are stored in Firebase with industry-standard encryption
  • No Account Required: The app does not require any account creation or login

Data Retention

  • On Your Device: Alarm data remains until you delete alarms or uninstall the app
  • Bug Reports: Stored in Firebase for up to 12 months for troubleshooting purposes
  • AI Messages: Not stored anywhere - generated and played in real-time only

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Your Name: You can change it anytime in app settings
  • Alarms: Delete any alarm data anytime
  • AI Features: Completely optional - you can choose not to use them
  • Bug Reports: Optional - submit only if you want to help improve the app
  • Uninstall: All local data is removed when you uninstall the app

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services Summary

  • Google Gemini AI: Used only when you enable AI wake-up messages
  • Firebase Firestore: Used only when you submit bug reports
  • No other third-party services are used
